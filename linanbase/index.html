A transaction is a regular part of a personâ€™s daily life. This is true for every place where transactions decide whether a company, group or a person earns or loses money in exchange for a product or service he or she is offered to or offers in return. Recording the daily transactions of a business is essential to keep track of the growth of the money invested in it.

In this case a company or a store would need to use a specific system to keep track of the sales on a day to day basis. These kinds of systems need specific requirements as per the need of the company. For the customers, this also provides a secure and reliable way for transactions since the transaction is then recorded. The system should have the capability to record items, date as well as the transaction.

One of the solutions to this sort of problem was created in 1986 which is called a Point of Sale system. A system that features an easy to use touch screen widget driven interface that allows for easier transactions both for the customer as well as the one managing said transaction.

Through a POS system, the seller as well as the customer is granted an easy way for transactions to occur. This is because a seller has easy access to the array of items he has if they are recorded in the system and the customer has the transaction recorded. Without a POS system, these transactions are less safe and less convenient both for the customer as well as the seller.
